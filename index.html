Museum Summit 2023 in Hong Kong

香港「博物館高峰論壇2023」

Dear friends,

We are delighted to announce that Museum Summit will come back in 2023 and will take place on 24 and 25 March during the period of Art Basel in Hong Kong. Presented by the Leisure and Cultural Services Department of the HKSAR Government (LCSD), Museum Summit 2023, the 3rd edition of the event, will be organised in partnership with the Gallerie degli Uffizi, Italy.

Pivoting on the theme of “Between”, the Summit 2023 would explore topics on cultural exchanges between places; the reinvention of old and new cultural facilities; the connection between museums and their communities; and the online and offline integration made possible by new technologies. We are honoured to have some 30 celebrated speakers from museums worldwide join us and share their insights and experiences.

Apart from the Museum Summit, participants would have opportunities to join other cultural events taking place around the same time. While the Art Basel in Hong Kong will take place on March 21 to 25, participants could also visit the newly opened Hong Kong Palace Museum and M+, along with the LCSD museums and exhibitions of other key cultural institutions. The Summit programme will include visits to these important venues. More information about the rich array of spectacular exhibitions and programmes will be updated in the Summit webpage nearer the time.

我們懷著興奮的心情通知大家第三屆「博物館高峰論壇」將於2023年3月24日至25日在香港舉行，並由香港特別行政區政府康樂及文化事務署(康文署)與意大利烏菲茲美術館攜手合作舉辦。

以「無間」為主題，高峰論壇2023內容將探討不同的課題，包括國際間的文化交流、新舊文化設施的再創造、博物館與社區的聯繫，以及科技發展帶來線上線下空間整合的新可能。我們很榮幸邀請到約30位來自世界各地博物館的著名講者前來分享他們的卓見和經驗。

香港文化界於2023年3月可謂熱鬧非常，除「博物館高峰論壇」外，巴賽爾藝術展香港展亦於3月21至25日舉行。此外，香港新成立的香港故宮文化博物館及M+博物館，以及康文署轄下博物館和其他多所主要文化機構亦為本地市民和旅客提供精彩的展覽和節目，詳情見以下網站。論壇節目將包括相關參觀活動，更多精彩内容請留意我們的網頁更新。
